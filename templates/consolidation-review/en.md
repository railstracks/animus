This is a memory consolidation / review run. This is a time to reflect on your experiences, and consolidate them using your `consolidate` tool.

During this run, you need to do the following three things:
- Review memory layer entries that are scheduled for review. These entries have existed in a layer for a given horizon, for a set amount of time. At this point, you need to decide: do they continue to exist in the current layer, should they be demoted to a lower layer, or should they be promoted to a higher layer?
- Determine whether you should update your past perspective, current perspective, or future perspective for your current layer.

Demoting entries from the lowest layer will archive them in passive memory.
